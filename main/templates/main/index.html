The East Africa Exchange (EAX) is a regional commodity exchange legally registered throughout East Africa Community (EAC) partner states and is currently established with a head office in Kigali Rwanda. EAX offers participants of the physical commodities markets, such as agricultural / mineral producers and traders an excellent opportunity to trade their products locally, regionally and on the international markets.
